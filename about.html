About

Tattoo Artist at Dream City Tattoo.


A self-taught artist, Dream City tattoo artist Javier Rivera A.K.A Simianart is all about pushing himself. Javier’s been tattooing since 2014 and loves the creative outlet it provides. His specialties are animated style, watercolor tattoos and Black & Grey.

When it comes to quality, Javier's advice is to choose wisely. “Good tattoos aren’t cheap, and cheap tattoos aren't good,” is his motto.

Chicago born and raised Javier Rivera hopes your first tattoo with him won’t be your last. He aims to provide the best experience possible and looks forward to seeing clients come back.
